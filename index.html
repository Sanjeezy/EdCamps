Welcome to EdCamps!

EdCamps is an educational company serving children in grades K-8. We put an emphasis on technology, and seek to provide children with an education and experience working with basic tools and technologies that will better prepare them to enter STEM fields. We offer a variety of different courses and try to expose kids to different areas of tech and let them pick the course that they find the most interesting. Courses typically last 1-2 weeks, and take place during the summer. See below to take a look at some of the courses we offer, and how to register!
